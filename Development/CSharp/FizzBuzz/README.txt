FizzBuzz
========
An extremely simple solution to the popular "FizzBuzz" interview question.

Purpose
=======
Prints the numbers 1 to 100 except in place of numbers that divide by 3 prints "Buzz", in place of numbers that divide by 5 prints "Fizz", and in place of numbers that divide by both 5 and 3 prints "FizzBuzz".

This solution is intentionally simple. It demonstrates the principle and one possible solution. There are many possible solutions and extensions to this concept.
